Intro

I am a postdoc researcher in astrophysics at the Johns Hokpkins University in U.S. I am mainly interested in studying large-scale outflows in galaxies and active galactic nuclei and studying cosmic reionization caused by low-mass, compact galaxies. I have been authored in more than 20 journal publications, and received more than 200 citations. I am in collaboration with world-wide experts in astronomy for cutting-edge projects from, e.g., Hubble Space Telescope , and James Webb Space Telescope. Check more information of me in this website!

Prior to that, I got my Ph.D. in astrophysics at Virginia Tech in U.S., and Bachelor degree in physics in USTC in China.
